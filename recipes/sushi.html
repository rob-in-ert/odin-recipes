Sushi

How to cook and assemble

Rinse and cook rice as normal (15-20 min). Sprinkle cooked rice with 1-2 tablespoons of rice vinegar, fluff, and set aside until assembly. Cut your sushi grade fish and other filler into appropriately sized pieces- to the length of the roll when possible.

To assemble, lay one sheet of seaweed shiny side down onto the sushi mat. Cover the entire sheet with rice, leaving about 1 top inch bare. Top the rice with filler ingredients, as well as the protein or other main ingredient. Roll the sushi from top to bottom, making sure to keep it tight. Wrap each roll in saran wrap and refridgerate until it is ready to cut. To cut use a serrated knife, moistening the blade before starting.

Ingredients

  • Sushi grade fish or other main ingredient
  • Sushi rice- 2 cups
  • Water- 4 cups
  • Rice vinegar
  • seaweed sheets- 5
  • Avocado, asparagus, cucumber, imitation crab...your choice
  • Sides- wasabi, ginger, soy sauce, sriracha mayo, etc

Steps

  1. Rinse and cook sushi rice, adding rice vinegar when done
  2. Cut filler and main ingredients into strips
  3. Place one sheet of seaweed shiny side down onto sushi mat
  4. Cover entire sheet with rice except for 1 inch strip at the top
  5. Top the rice with desired ingredients
  6. Roll the sushi top to bottom, keeping it as tight as possibe
  7. Wrap each roll tightly with saran wrap and refridgerate until ready to cut and serve
  8. Moisten serrated knife blade and cut into 8 pieces, service with desired sides
